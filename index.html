About Me

Hey! I'm Jericho, an independent developer. I build tools, mods, bots, and utilities. Sometimes I live-code projects from scratch on Twitch - break things, fix things, document it. I love tackling weird programming shenanigans, and I'm also a gamer, spending most of my time in Microsoft Flight Simulator, with the occasional detour into Halo, Minecraft, and survival-horror games.

My goal is simple: create fun, useful, or just plain interesting stuff for people to enjoy or learn from.

"There's nothing quite like being able to say at the end of the day, I made this. This is my world." – me

What I'm up to
Building games with LÖVE, Solar2D and LibGDX
Modding worlds in Minecraft, Halo, Roblox, and Garry's Mod
Building community tools like Discord bots, PWAs, and mobile apps
Maintaining HALO-SCRIPT-PROJECTS - the largest collection of Lua scripts for Halo: PC/CE
Running Coastal Peaks Air Service (CPAS) - a Virtual Charter (VC) and specialty flight operator for Microsoft Flight Simulator. We operate general aviation aircraft and helicopters for tours, charters, and HEMS missions, flying realistic routes across New Zealand's South Island. Our flights cover Canterbury, Christchurch, Banks Peninsula, and the dramatic landscapes of the West Coast and Southern Alps, offering an experience distinct from traditional virtual airlines.
