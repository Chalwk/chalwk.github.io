PGR2 Discord Bot

This bot is specifically designed to enhance the gaming experience of Project Gotham Racing 2 fans by providing randomized game settings for exciting and unique challenges.

Features:

  • /pgr2 class: Generates a random vehicle class to keep your gameplay fresh.
  • /pgr2 vehicle: Provides a random vehicle from the selected class.
  • /pgr2 track: Selects a random track from the PGR2 world.
  • /pgr2 time: Determines a random time of day setting.
  • /pgr2 weather: Randomizes in-game weather conditions.
  • /pgr2 all: Generates a combination of random settings for ultimate gameplay variety.
