About Me

Hello! I'm Chalwk, a passionate independent game developer and open-source enthusiast from New Zealand.

My journey began with creating simple games at a young age. Over the years, I've honed my skills in 2D game development and explored various coding projects, from software engineering to innovative R&D.

I thrive on challenges and love pushing creativity through creative coding. Whether developing immersive gameplay or contributing to open-source, I aim to make technology accessible and engaging.

When not coding, I explore gaming concepts, read about tech, or enjoy the outdoors with my daughter.
