About Me

I'm Chalwk, an independent game developer and open-source enthusiast based in New Zealand. With a passion for technology, I specialize in developing 2D games and engaging in a variety of coding projects, ranging from software engineering to research and development, and creative coding.

As a polymath, I have a keen interest in areas such as aerospace, mathematics, computer programming, and arachnology. My experiences have shaped my approach to problem-solving and creativity in my projects, allowing me to cultivate a unique perspective in both game development and technology.

I am dedicated to raising autism awareness and actively share insights and knowledge through my work and community involvement. You can explore my projects, including Java plugins, Discord bots, and various Lua scripts for gaming communities, here on my site.

My Projects

  • Halo Script Projects

    A comprehensive collection of high-quality Lua scripts for SAPP (Sapphire), designed to enhance gameplay in Halo PC/CE. This repository showcases my most prolific work in the Halo community, featuring various scripts that provide new functionalities, gameplay improvements, and engaging experiences for players.

  • BigBrother Minecraft Plugin

    A versatile plugin for Minecraft that introduces unique features and enhancements for server management. It allows server administrators to monitor player activity and offers tools for improving gameplay experiences.

  • Hangman Discord Bot

    An interactive Discord bot that allows users to play the classic word-guessing game Hangman. It features various difficulty levels and customization options, making it a fun addition to any server.

  • Tic-Tac-Toe Game Bot

    A Discord bot that allows users to engage in Tic-Tac-Toe matches within their server. It features easy-to-use commands and provides a friendly interface for players.

  • Minesweeper Discord Bot

    A fun and interactive Discord bot that brings the classic game of Minesweeper to your server. Users can compete against each other in this strategic game, making it an exciting addition for gaming communities.

  • PGR2 Discord Bot

    A fun and engaging Discord bot written in Java utilizing the JDA library. This bot enhances the gaming experience of Project Gotham Racing 2 fans by providing randomized game settings for unique challenges.

  • Halo Discord Bot

    This bot serves as a custom bridge between Halo 1 (PC/CE) and Discord, enhancing the gaming experience by keeping your Discord community up-to-date with in-game events. It sends customizable event notifications to Discord channels, making it easy for server members to stay informed about what's happening in the game.
