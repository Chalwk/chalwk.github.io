About Me

I'm Chalwk, an independent game developer and open-source enthusiast based in New Zealand. With a passion for technology, I specialize in developing 2D games and engaging in a variety of coding projects, ranging from software engineering to research and development, and creative coding.

As a polymath, I have a keen interest in areas such as aerospace, mathematics, computer programming, and arachnology. My experiences have shaped my approach to problem-solving and creativity in my projects, allowing me to cultivate a unique perspective in both game development and technology.

I am dedicated to raising autism awareness and actively share insights and knowledge through my work and community involvement. You can explore my projects, including Java plugins, Discord bots, and various Lua scripts for gaming communities, here on my site.
