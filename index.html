About Me

Kia ora! I'm Chalwk (also known as Jay), a self-taught developer with a passion for game development, modding, and Discord bot creation. I love tackling technical challenges and puzzle-solving through code.

My work spans projects for Minecraft, Halo, Roblox, and Garry's Mod. I was recognised in 2021 as the most prolific SAPP Lua scripter for Halo PC/CE, authoring the HALO-SCRIPT-PROJECTS repository - the largest public archive of SAPP and Phasor Lua scripts, with nearly 3K commits and 20+ major releases. I've also developed 100+ private Lua scripts for Halo clan servers and maintain a large collection of Chimera scripts.

In Minecraft, I've created numerous custom plugins, including BigBrother (admin utility), Magnet, Night Vision, furniture mods, and Slimefun4 addons. I also ran JeriCraft, a medieval-themed public server from 2013 to May 2025.

I use GitHub for version control, favour IntelliJ IDEA Community for coding, and have experience with a range of languages and formats, proficient in Lua, Java, JSON, XML, YAML, and CSV, with additional experience in C++, PHP, HTML, CSS, and SQL.
