Overview

The Uber Racing experience combines intense team-based racing with advanced systems. It's not just about being the fastest driver, it's about teamwork, strategy, and leveraging multiple tactical systems to dominate the race.

Key Features

  • On-Demand Vehicle Spawning: Type keywords like hog or rhog in chat to instantly spawn a vehicle in front of you and automatically enter the driver's seat.
  • The Uber System: Use chat commands like uber or taxi to instantly join a teammate's vehicle. Perfect for recovering from wipeouts or coordinating team movements.
  • Deployable Mines: Place tactical explosives from your vehicle to create defensive traps for pursuing enemies.
  • Race Enforcement: Automated system ensures fair play by requiring players to stay in vehicles during races.
  • Advanced Lap Tracking: Comprehensive system tracks lap times, validates laps, and maintains personal bests and all-time map records.
  • Global Rankings: Weighted point system ranks players across all maps based on performance and records.
  • Massive Vehicle Support: Extensive vehicle whitelist supporting countless custom maps with proper seat configurations.
  • Team-Based Strategy: Coordinate convoys with gunners defending while drivers focus on racing.
