Overview

One In The Chamber is a tense, precision-based game mode where players are equipped with a pistol and only one bullet. Every shot counts, as a single shot can eliminate your opponent. If you miss, you are left with melee combat until you get another kill. For every opponent you eliminate, you are rewarded with one bullet. The success of this mode depends on your combination of precision, reflexes, and knowing when to take the shot or close in for the kill.

Key Features

  • Pistol-Only: Players start with a single bullet in their pistol.
  • Melee Combat on Miss: If you miss, you must resort to melee combat until you get another kill.
  • Ammo Per Kill: Successfully eliminating an enemy rewards you with a bullet for your pistol.
  • No Grenades, No Vehicles: Grenades and vehicles are completely disabled in this mode.
  • Tactical Play: It's all about precision and timing, you need to know when to shoot and when to close in for a melee kill.
