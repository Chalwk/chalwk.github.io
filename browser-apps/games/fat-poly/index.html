FatPoly

Eat healthy shapes, avoid unhealthy ones. Reach level 10 to win!

Controls:

Arrow Up/W: Move up
Arrow Down/S: Move down
Arrow Left/A: Move left
Arrow Right/D: Move right
Click/Drag: Move player
Space: Pause/Resume
Score: 0
Level: 1
Health:
Eaten: 0 / Target: 10
