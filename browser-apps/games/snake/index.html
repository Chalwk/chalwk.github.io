Snake

Fast. Fancy. Frenetic.

Tip: Golden apples are worth a lot. Powerups are temporary but game changing.

How to play

  • Eat food to grow and score points.
  • Use powerups for short advantages.
  • Avoid colliding with yourself and (depending on mode) walls or obstacles.
  • Pause with Space or the Pause button. Touch controls supported.
